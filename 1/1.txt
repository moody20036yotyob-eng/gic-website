In January 2025, The Game Innovation Center also hosted the Global Game Jam, a 3-day event where over 120 participants formed teams to develop games from scratch, guided by the theme "Bubbles!". The teams showcased their game prototypes, with 33 presenting playable versions. The jam showed the amazing potential of games in education, fostered the local game development community in the Kingdom and emphasized the blend of creativity engineering and game design expertise.
