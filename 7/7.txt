In collaboration with Nesma Group, the GIC hosted a PUBGM World of Wonder (WoW) Booth, giving students a hands-on opportunity to explore map design. Using the WoW platform, participants could create and customize their own game modes, experimenting with terrain, obstacles, and interactive features, and then share them for others to play. The event also featured a small competition with a cash prize, adding excitement and friendly rivalry among participants. Overall, it encouraged creativity, innovation, and collaboration, allowing students to experience the cycle of game development and gain practical skills in designing engaging gaming environments.
