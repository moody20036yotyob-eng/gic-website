Moreover, Game Innovation Center arranged “Maze of Hope” event that was sponsored by Boeing from May 13th to 15th. This charity initiative, aimed at supporting children with cancer, showcased the talent and compassion of Alfaisal students introducing a VR maze game, developed in collaboration with by UpDown Studio, a startup founded by Alfaisal University students. The event received immense support from various university departments and student associations. The success of the Maze of Hope event underscores the potential of technology for social good, emphasizing collaboration, innovation, and the dedication of the students, leaving a lasting impact on children battling cancer and inspiring students to contribute positively to society.
