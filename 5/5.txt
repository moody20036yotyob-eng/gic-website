The Annual Mini-Games & ProGames Exhibit, held from December 8 to 10, 2024, highlighted the creativity and technical growth of students in the newly launched Game Development and Digital Media Track at Alfaisal University. With over 130 participants and 35 projects on display, the event brought together sophomore and senior students showcasing their work across categories such as Creative Execution, Esports, Narrative and Design, and Games for Good. Supported by industry partners including MSI, Immerstec VR, Qandeel Studio, Shafra Games, 6wrni.com, and Updown Studio. Organized under the leadership of the Game Innovation Center, the exhibit underscored the track’s strong foundations in programming, game engine development, and design, while emphasizing the growing role of partnerships in advancing game development education at Alfaisal
