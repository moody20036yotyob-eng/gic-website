Program Description

In partnership with Savvy Gaming Group, Week 2 of the RoboGames Academy will focus on turning players into makers! We will explore the pipeline of production of a game — from Game Design, to Animation, Narrative, Publishing, and more — and together build a unique and fully playable video game.

RoboGames & Esports Academy will focus on the dynamic world of esports, providing students with certified training in competitive gaming and education in map building. This program is the first of its kind in the Kingdom designed specifically for high school students. Participants will gain insights into the fast-paced world of professional gaming, learning from industry experts while engaging in hands-on practice with cutting-edge tools and platforms.

RoboGames is an extended edition of the MobiSiTech+ and AR Games Developer tracks offered from 2020 to 2023, which reflected a huge success. The track introduces mobile game apps, Artificial Intelligence in games, Unity for 2D and 3D environments, 3D modeling, Game Design and Art making, Animation, and Playful Robotics development with special focus on Extended Reality (XR).

Students will implement gaming applications that serve various real-life sectors (educational, business, entertainment, gaming). They will also see how mobile apps interact with robotics and dive into the entertainment world throughout hands-on workshops with field experts.

Figure 1: Program students winning first place in Gameathon Riyadh, Summer 2022.

The program offers the first global training experience in the Kingdom on esports and allows students to gain free membership in Alfaisal's Game Innovation Lab — growing their passion in game innovation by being part of future impactful projects that match their interest.

It also opens the space for students to work on activities, meet gaming communities, and participate in global annual challenges like Game Jams, Gameathon, the Mini-Games Exhibit, and Robotics Challenges at Alfaisal during and after the program.

Eng. Hoda Ahmed Galal Elsayed is a Software Engineering Lecturer at Alfaisal University, specialized in web, mobile, and game development applications with AI, IoT, and XR. She has served as a Session Chair at HCI International and an invited speaker and panelist at the VoV and AGI Conferences. As an ACM and Springer-published researcher, she has contributed to VR, AI, cybersecurity, and persuasive computing. She has led training programs on AR in Banking (Bank AlJazira), Storytelling & Game Design (Game Bootcamp with Misk & DGA), and XR Bootcamp (Saudi Esports Federation). She serves as Game Innovation Center Director, Robogames & Esports Academy Director, IEEE Student Branch Counselor, and QA Coordinator. Her work has earned multiple awards including 1st Place Boeing Engineering Competition, 2nd place Arabnet Ideathon, and Best Research & Poster Awards.
