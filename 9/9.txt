In May 2025, the lab held and executed the Alfaisal Gaming Innovation Conference, a 2 day event bringing speakers from both the local and international game development community, serving as a dynamic platform for industry leaders, innovators, and enthusiasts to converge, collaborate, and catalyze the Kingdom's gaming ecosystem. Inviting 40+ speakers and 250+ Game Innovators. Furthermore, the event hosted multiple workshops and teaching sessions on the topics of Esports, Content Creation, Blockchain & AI in Gaming, Immersive Experiences (VR & MR) as well as Game Development & Design.
