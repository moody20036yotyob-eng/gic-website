About GIC

Built to Push Every Boundary

The Game Innovation Center is a hybrid educational hub and innovative research unit at Alfaisal University's College of Engineering, focused on game research, innovation, design, and development. We explore the possibilities of play and enable game creators to drive real-world change — using games that help people learn, improve their communities, and contribute to making the world a better place. Our fully equipped game studio hosts game jams, competitions, research projects, and collaborations with industry partners across education, healthcare, entertainment, and strategic sectors using VR, AR, and gaming robotics.

Facilities

Our Space

Industry-grade environments built so you graduate ready — not just educated.

🥽

XR Prototyping Lab

Industry-grade VR/AR headsets — Meta Quest, HTC Vive, Microsoft HoloLens. Six full development stations with haptic feedback rigs.

Meta Quest HTC Vive HoloLens
🎮

Esports Arena

20-seat competitive arena with 240Hz gaming monitors, mechanical keyboards, and professional streaming infrastructure for tournament broadcasts.

240Hz Displays Live Stream 20 Seats
🎥

Motion Capture Studio

Full-body optical motion capture with 12-camera array. Directly export to Unity and Unreal Engine — animate characters with real human movement.

12-Cam Array Unity Export Unreal Export
🖥

Game Dev Studios

30 high-spec workstations with RTX 4090s, dual-monitor setups, and licensed seats of Unity, Unreal, Maya, and Adobe Creative Suite.

RTX 4090 30 Stations Full Licenses
🤖

Robotics Bay

Dedicated Robogames workspace with modular arenas, micro-controllers, servo kits, 3D printers, and laser cutters for rapid prototyping.

3D Printing Laser Cutter Live Arena
🎥

Broadcast & Recording

Soundproofed podcast and game streaming booth with 4K cameras, LED lighting rigs, and professional audio mixing. Used for events and content creation.

4K Cameras Soundproofed Live Mix
